FAQ


I am having issues with my account

Please ensure that the username and password used are correct.
If you are having trouble logging in, please email origame@gmail.com for further assistance.

How does the point system work?

Points are earned per completion of each tutorial or challenges!
Make sure to collect as much points as possible as you can redeem it for rewards through this page!

Will points expire?

Yes!
Points expire with a duration of 1 year from the time you earned it, so make sure to use them up!

I want to make a return for the items I have purchased.

We're so sorry to hear that, please email origame@gmail.com to make your return.

What is OrigaME?

OrigaME! aims to spread the joy of creating origami through gamified system of exchanging points for rewards.
For more information, you can check out our About page or drop us a message!
