About Us

Formed in 2024, origaME! is a platform application that offers tutorials for everyone to follow and create. It provides a wide range of origami tutorials, catering to beginners and advanced users alike. With a user-friendly interface and step-by-step instructions, origaME! aims to make origami accessible and enjoyable for all ages. Whether you're new to origami or looking to expand your skills, origaME! has something for everyone.

Our Goal

Our goal is to enable children to delight in learning traditional activities. The initiative challenges the perception that introducing technology to children at a young age is detrimental. Instead, OrigaME aims to counter this stereotype by providing a safe and enjoyable way for children to engage with technology while learning and having fun. OrigaME endeavors to serve as a platform where children can be accompanied and nurtured, guiding children to develop a variety of both soft and hard skills as they grow.

Our Vision

Our vision at origaME! is to revolutionize the way children learn and engage with traditional arts, such as origami, through gamification. Considering that children are at an age where exploring interests and engaging in hobbies is customary, acquiring new skills can profoundly influence them. Introducing a gamified approach to learning the step-by-step instructions of origami could effectively convey the idea that education and acquiring knowledge can be enjoyable and thrilling for them. We envision a future where every child has access to a fun and interactive platform that not only teaches them valuable skills but also sparks their creativity and curiosity. By combining the art of origami with the excitement of gaming, we aim to create a dynamic learning environment that inspires a lifelong love for learning and creativity in children worldwide.
